how it works

Zebrango Price Prediction is a fun and simple decentralized Game.
Predict if the price of AVAX/USD will be higher or lower after each round.

example:

- if you bet that the price of AVAX/USD will go up and the closed price is higher than the locked price after the live round finishes, you WIN!
- if you bet that the price of AVAX/USD will go down and the closed price is lower than the locked price after the live round finishes, you WIN!


How is the payout calculated?
Payout ratio for UP pool = total value of both pools รท value of UP pool.
Payout ratio for DOWN pool = total value of both pools รท value of DOWN pool.


What are the fees?
4% of each round total prize, which will be used completley to fund Zebrango events.


What are you using for the price feed?
Our smart contract uses Chainlink oracle to get the latest price feed.


Why the game is paused?
This means the game smart contract is unable to obtain the price from chainlink oracle at the time, the round has ended.


When will the game resume after being paused?
The game will resume when the operator manually resumes the game.


What will happen to my funds if the game paused?
If the game pauses while you have a live position, your funds will be available to reclaim.


What will happen if the price didn't move?
This is a very rare thing to happen, but if it did happen, all funds will be send to the Zebrango events.
